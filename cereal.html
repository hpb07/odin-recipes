Cereal

Cereal is a classic breakfast option with a history as rich as it's flavor.

Here's what you'll need:

  • Bowl
  • Cereal of your choice
  • Milk of your choice
  • Spoon

Here are the steps to making the perfect bowl of cereal.

  1. Grab a bowl
  2. Pour cereal in bowl
  3. Pour milk in bowl (don't overfill!)
  4. Serve with spoon and enjoy!
