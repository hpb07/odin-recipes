Pizza

Pizza is a classic lunch option with a history as rich as it's flavor.

Here's what you'll need:

  • Premade dough
  • Pizza sauce of your choice
  • Toppings of your choice
  • Pizza pan
  • Oven

Here are the steps to making the perfect pizza.

  1. Grab a pizza pan
  2. Put premade dough on pan and spread it out evenly.
  3. Put sauce and toppings onto the dough.
  4. Heat in oven at 400 degrees Fahrenheit for 20-25 minutes (watch closely, results may vary).
  5. Serve and enjoy! Cut it into as many pieces as you like. It will always be an even amount of slices unless you remove some slices.
