Protein Shake

Protein shakes are a classic snack option with a history as rich as it's flavors.

Here's what you'll need:

  • A cup
  • Protein powder of your choice
  • Water or milk of your choice
  • Spoon

Here are the steps to making the perfect protein shake.

  1. Grab a cup.
  2. Pour protein powder in the cup.
  3. Pour water or milk in the cup (don't overfill!)
  4. Stir with spoon and enjoy!

Pro tip: Use a shaker bottle for better results!
